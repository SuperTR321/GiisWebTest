What Is Giis Smart Campus?

Giis Campus also known as Global Indian International School Smart Campus is a school which includes many studies from technoligy. It has many activities. The school has many events such as art, crafts, making stories, sports and competing other schools. There are more than 92 classrooms in giis and about 7 senimar halls! The school has many common areas, where there are different shapes of sofas. There are 2 blocks in giis! Primary Block and secondary block. The primary block has 6 floors and the secondary block has 7 floors. There is a big bridge in the school! Its known as then friendship bridge. Go to the topic "Friendship Bridge" to know more. Giis is a very big school!
